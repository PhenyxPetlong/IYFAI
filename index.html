About Innovative youths for Africa

IYFAI

Innovative youths for Africa (IYFAI) is a nonprofit organization designed to bring all Africans in diaspora around the world together to discuss the way forward in Africa . IYFA wants to take Africa out of the dominant powers to the promise land where we see Africa fending for itself in all aspect of our economy. Innovative Youth for Africa Initiative (IYFAI) is an African NGO made up of a network of Africans youths in diaspora working to bring back ideas and opportunities to develop the continent. IYFAI was founded in 2015 and is aimed at implementing innovative ideas for community development, contributing both to the economy and to humanity. Currently the organization is operational in a few countries in Africa and we are looking to cover the entire Africa by the help of volunteers and partnerships. IYFAI is the change force that will enhance multiple creation of our own inventions ranging from ICT software, textile, autos, equipments, technical know-how and many more.

IYFAI

We project an African Hub of outstanding economy duplicated to every single country in Africa. IYFAI will not leave any stone unturned in the development of Africa. We have also made censored plans to connect our rural communities to the international markets to leverage on the robust economic development in all the areas of economy. IYFAI is the African development forum ready to take African economy to the next level of development. We shall be working with many African experts in conjunction with other experts from other continents. IYFA is however connecting with African governments and NGOs across the continents to help deploy this development in respective areas for visibility.

IYFAI

VISION
To bring African youths together from all over the world and involve them in the development of Africa.

OUR MISSION
To build Africa with innovative ideas to make Africa a better continent for the future generation through capacity building, research and development in all African countries. It is clearly seen that despite the immense resources and knowledge endowed in Africa, not even a 5th of its potentials are being explored and this in turn reflects on the pace of development compared to the tremendous speed of development in this current age. Our network are both experienced and passionate about participating in societal development with the experience gained to lead the future generation into a path of sustainable development. It is in the recognition of the need to develop Africa up to the potential of its resources to build a sustainable and strong society for the generations to come.
